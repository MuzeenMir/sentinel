SENTINEL — Watch-test Quickstart (v0.1.1)
==========================================

You're testing a pre-release build. Five steps. Two scripts.

1. RIGHT-CLICK PowerShell -> "Run as administrator".
2. cd into this folder (where you unzipped the bundle).
3. Run:    .\setup.ps1
4. Use your computer normally for a while. Browse, work, whatever.
   If you hit a malicious site, you'll see a Sentinel block-page.
5. When you want to stop testing, run:    .\uninstall.ps1
   That puts your DNS back the way it was.

If something looks broken or you just want to undo:
  .\uninstall.ps1   (always safe to run, even if setup didn't finish)

Questions during the test? Ask the person who handed you this folder.
They'll be sitting with you for the first 30 minutes.

That's it. No internet account, no telemetry, no auto-update.
